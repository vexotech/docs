# Billing

Manage your billing information, payment methods, and invoices.

## Overview

The Billing section allows you to manage all financial aspects of your Vexo Analytics account, including payment methods, invoices, and billing history.

## Current Plan

### Plan Details
- **Current Plan**: View your active subscription plan
- **Plan Features**: See included features and limits
- **Usage Statistics**: Monitor your current usage vs plan limits
- **Next Billing Date**: When your next payment is due
- **Plan Cost**: Monthly or annual billing amount

### Usage Monitoring
- **API Calls**: Track your API usage against plan limits
- **Data Storage**: Monitor data storage consumption
- **Session Replays**: Track session replay usage
- **Custom Events**: Monitor custom event tracking usage

## Payment Methods

### Manage Payment Methods
- **Primary Payment Method**: Set your default payment source
- **Add Payment Method**: Add credit/debit cards or bank accounts
- **Update Card Information**: Change expiration dates or billing addresses
- **Remove Payment Methods**: Delete unused payment methods

### Supported Payment Types
- **Credit Cards**: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- **Debit Cards**: Most major debit cards accepted
- **Bank Transfers**: ACH transfers for enterprise customers
- **Wire Transfers**: Available for large enterprise accounts

### Payment Security
- **PCI Compliance**: All payments processed securely
- **Encrypted Storage**: Payment information is encrypted
- **Fraud Protection**: Advanced fraud detection systems

## Billing Information

### Billing Address
- **Company Name**: Your business name for invoices
- **Billing Address**: Street address for billing
- **City, State, ZIP**: Complete billing location
- **Country**: Billing country for tax calculations
- **Tax ID/VAT**: Business tax identification numbers

### Invoice Preferences
- **Invoice Email**: Who receives billing notifications
- **Invoice Format**: PDF or electronic format preference
- **Purchase Order**: Add PO numbers to invoices
- **Billing Contact**: Designated billing contact person

## Invoices & History

### Recent Invoices
- **View Invoices**: Access your recent billing statements
- **Download PDF**: Get printable invoice copies
- **Payment Status**: See payment confirmation status
- **Invoice Details**: Detailed breakdown of charges

### Payment History
- **Transaction History**: Complete payment history
- **Failed Payments**: View and retry failed transactions
- **Refunds**: Track refund requests and status
- **Credits**: Account credits and promotional balances

### Billing Notifications
- **Payment Confirmations**: Receive payment receipts
- **Payment Failures**: Get notified of payment issues
- **Invoice Reminders**: Upcoming payment notifications
- **Plan Limit Warnings**: Usage approaching plan limits

## Plan Changes

### Upgrade Your Plan
- **Compare Plans**: See available plan options
- **Instant Upgrades**: Upgrade immediately with prorated billing
- **Feature Comparison**: Compare features across plans
- **Contact Sales**: Get help choosing the right plan

### Downgrade Options
- **Downgrade Process**: Steps to reduce your plan
- **Data Retention**: Understand data retention during downgrades
- **Feature Loss**: See which features you'll lose
- **Effective Date**: When plan changes take effect

## Tax Information

### Tax Calculations
- **Sales Tax**: Automatically calculated based on location
- **VAT**: European VAT handling for EU customers
- **GST**: Goods and Services Tax for applicable regions
- **Tax Exemptions**: Upload tax exemption certificates

### Tax Compliance
- **Tax Invoices**: Compliant invoicing for business customers
- **Tax Reports**: Annual tax summaries for accounting
- **Regional Compliance**: Adherence to local tax regulations

## Enterprise Billing

### Enterprise Features
- **Annual Billing**: Discounted annual payment options
- **Custom Invoicing**: Tailored invoicing for large organizations
- **Net Payment Terms**: Extended payment terms (Net 30, Net 60)
- **Purchase Orders**: Custom PO processing

### Volume Discounts
- **Usage-Based Pricing**: Discounts for high-volume usage
- **Multi-Year Discounts**: Savings for longer commitments
- **Custom Pricing**: Negotiated pricing for enterprise needs

## Billing Support

### Get Help
- **Billing Questions**: Contact billing support team
- **Dispute Resolution**: Process for billing disputes
- **Payment Issues**: Get help with payment problems
- **Plan Guidance**: Assistance choosing the right plan

### Contact Information
- **Billing Support**: billing@vexo.co
- **Phone Support**: Available for premium customers
- **Live Chat**: Real-time billing assistance
- **Help Center**: Self-service billing documentation
