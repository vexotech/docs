# Account Settings

Manage your personal account information and preferences.

## Overview

Account Settings allow you to control your personal profile, security preferences, and account-specific configurations.

## Profile Information

### Personal Details
- **Full Name**: Update your display name
- **Email Address**: Change your primary email (requires verification)
- **Profile Picture**: Upload or change your avatar
- **Job Title**: Add your role for team identification
- **Company**: Update your organization information

### Contact Preferences
- **Primary Email**: Set your main contact email
- **Secondary Email**: Add backup email for important notifications
- **Phone Number**: Add phone for account security (optional)
- **Communication Language**: Choose your preferred language

## Security Settings

### Password Management
- **Change Password**: Update your account password
- **Password Requirements**: Must be at least 8 characters with special characters
- **Password History**: Cannot reuse last 5 passwords
- **Password Expiration**: Optional 90-day password rotation

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- **Enable 2FA**: Add extra security to your account
- **Authenticator Apps**: Use Google Authenticator, Authy, or similar
- **Backup Codes**: Generate recovery codes for emergency access
- **SMS Backup**: Optional SMS-based backup authentication

### Account Recovery
- **Recovery Email**: Set backup email for account recovery
- **Security Questions**: Configure security questions
- **Recovery Phone**: Add phone number for account recovery

## Team & Permissions

### Organization Membership
- **Current Organizations**: View all organizations you belong to
- **Role in Organization**: See your permissions level
- **Team Invitations**: Manage pending team invitations

### Access Permissions
- **Dashboard Access**: Configure which dashboards you can view
- **Data Export**: Set permissions for data export capabilities
- **Admin Functions**: View administrative permissions
- **API Access**: Manage API key generation permissions

## Privacy Preferences

### Data Sharing
- **Analytics Sharing**: Control data sharing with team members
- **Usage Statistics**: Share anonymous usage data with Vexo
- **Marketing Communications**: Opt in/out of promotional emails
- **Product Updates**: Receive notifications about new features

### Account Visibility
- **Profile Visibility**: Control who can see your profile
- **Activity Status**: Show/hide when you're online
- **Recent Activity**: Display your recent dashboard activity

## Account Management

### Account Status
- **Account Type**: View your current account type (Free, Pro, Enterprise)
- **Account Creation Date**: See when your account was created
- **Last Login**: View your recent login activity
- **Active Sessions**: See all active login sessions

### Data Management
- **Export Personal Data**: Download all your personal data
- **Data Deletion**: Request deletion of personal data
- **Account Backup**: Create backup of your account settings

## Danger Zone

### Account Actions
- **Deactivate Account**: Temporarily disable your account
- **Delete Account**: Permanently delete your account and all data
- **Transfer Ownership**: Transfer account ownership to another user
