# Subscription

Manage your Vexo Analytics subscription plan and features.

## Overview

The Subscription section provides detailed control over your Vexo Analytics plan, including feature management, usage monitoring, and plan modifications.

## Current Subscription

### Plan Information
- **Plan Name**: Your current subscription tier (Starter, Professional, Enterprise)
- **Billing Cycle**: Monthly or annual billing
- **Subscription Status**: Active, canceled, or suspended
- **Auto-Renewal**: Whether your plan auto-renews
- **Contract End Date**: When your current commitment expires

### Subscription ID
- **Subscription ID**: Unique identifier for support requests
- **Account Manager**: Your dedicated contact (Enterprise plans)
- **Support Level**: Your included support tier

## Plan Features

### Included Features
- **Monthly Page Views**: Your plan's page view allowance
- **Data Retention**: How long data is stored
- **API Calls**: Monthly API request limit
- **Session Replays**: Number of session recordings included
- **Custom Events**: Custom event tracking limit
- **Team Members**: Maximum users on your account

### Feature Usage
- **Current Usage**: Real-time usage statistics
- **Usage Trends**: Historical usage patterns
- **Projected Usage**: Estimated monthly consumption
- **Overage Alerts**: Notifications when approaching limits

## Available Plans

### Starter Plan - $29/month
- **Page Views**: Up to 100K monthly page views
- **Data Retention**: 12 months
- **API Calls**: 1M monthly requests
- **Session Replays**: 1,000 monthly sessions
- **Team Members**: Up to 3 users
- **Support**: Email support

### Professional Plan - $99/month
- **Page Views**: Up to 1M monthly page views
- **Data Retention**: 24 months
- **API Calls**: 10M monthly requests
- **Session Replays**: 10,000 monthly sessions
- **Custom Events**: Unlimited
- **Team Members**: Up to 10 users
- **Support**: Priority email + chat support

### Enterprise Plan - Custom Pricing
- **Page Views**: Unlimited
- **Data Retention**: Custom (up to 7 years)
- **API Calls**: Unlimited
- **Session Replays**: Unlimited
- **Custom Features**: Custom dashboard widgets
- **Team Members**: Unlimited
- **Support**: Dedicated account manager + phone support

## Plan Management

### Upgrade Your Plan
- **Instant Upgrades**: Immediate access to new features
- **Prorated Billing**: Pay only for the remaining billing period
- **Feature Activation**: New features enabled immediately
- **Data Migration**: Seamless transition of existing data

### Downgrade Considerations
- **Feature Loss**: Features removed with lower plans
- **Data Retention**: Potential data loss with shorter retention
- **User Limits**: May need to remove team members
- **Effective Date**: Changes take effect at next billing cycle

### Plan Customization (Enterprise)
- **Custom Limits**: Negotiate specific usage allowances
- **Feature Additions**: Add specialized features
- **Integration Support**: Custom integration assistance
- **Training**: Dedicated onboarding and training
